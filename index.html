About Me:

I am currently the supervisor of a large format print room at a sign shop. After starting out as a shop floater I began offering help in the print room as I had previous experience with Adobe Illustrator which was being used for the equipment. After dedicating my time to learning the equipment and software I made it to my current position. The use of adobe-egnines for the majority of my work, and RIP software for images is very interesting to me. This led to some online research about writing code and how it is used for different applications and eventually me signing up for a full stack bootcamp! Through my work in the bootcamp I hope to gain the skills necessary to land a job as a software developer and begin building a new career. The process of using technical-knowledge, creativity, and strategy to solve problems sounds like a dream job. Especially so in an ever-expanding field!
